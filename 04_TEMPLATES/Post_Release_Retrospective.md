## Lessons Learned

*Summarize 2–4 durable lessons that should change how the team plans, tests, or communicates for future releases. Be specific — "test payment retries under network loss before every release touching billing" is useful; "be more careful" is not.*

## Follow-Up

- [ ] Action items entered into backlog/tracker with owners and due dates.
- [ ] Risk Register and Bug Triage Matrix updated with recurring patterns for future scoping.

- [ ] Retro archived alongside the release snapshot for future reference.
