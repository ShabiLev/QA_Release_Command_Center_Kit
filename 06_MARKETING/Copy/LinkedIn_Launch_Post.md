# LinkedIn Launch Post

I built a product for QA Managers who are tired of running releases based on scattered updates and last-minute pressure.

It is called **QA Release Command Center Kit**.

It combines:
- A modular local Command Center
- Multi-project release tracking
- Risk, bug, regression, sign-off, and deployment readiness management
- AI prompts for release analysis and communication
- Specialized templates for mobile, API, data/SQL, and AI products

The goal is simple:
Move from “we think it is ready” to “we can show what is ready, what is risky, and whether this should actually go live.”

If you manage releases, QA, or product delivery, this was built for you.
